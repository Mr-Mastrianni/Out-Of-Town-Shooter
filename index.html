The Book

Every city has a shadow. This one has a name.

When a out-of-town hitter takes a contract in a city that doesn't forgive outsiders, one job spirals into a war between rival factions, crooked cops, and a past he thought he buried six feet deep.

Out of Town Shooter pulls you through the grimy back alleys and neon-lit corners of a city where loyalty is currency and betrayal comes with a body count. No witness protection. No way out. Just survival.

Raw. Unflinching. Unforgettable.

"A bullet doesn't care where you're from. It only cares where you're standing."
— Out of Town Shooter
The Author

Voice of the streets.

James Wood brings the raw energy of the city to every page. With a voice forged in the neighborhoods most writers only drive past, he delivers fiction that hits like a shot of adrenaline — real, uncompromising, and impossible to put down.

Out of Town Shooter is his debut novel.
